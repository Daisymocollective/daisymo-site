Open Studio · Photo · Video · Events

The main studio at DaisyMo Collective is an open room designed to work like a blank canvas. Everything inside can be moved and rearranged — furniture, props, and sets — so the space shifts with your vision.

It's intentionally minimal, modern, and open enough to support photoshoots, video work, trainings, small gatherings, meetings, mixers, or whatever creative project you're building.

Nothing is fixed or overly staged. You walk in, shape the room the way you want, and create without restrictions. The layout stays clean, the lighting is effortless, and the atmosphere is calm and polished.

Space 03

DMC Podcast
Room

Audio · Video · Self-Service Recording

The podcast room at DaisyMo Collective is designed to give creators a clean, modern, and stress-free recording experience. The space centers around a broadcast-quality audio setup powered by the Rodecaster Pro II, paired with professional microphones, comfortable seating, and soft LED lighting.

For creators who want more than audio, a single-camera video option is mounted behind a teleprompter, so you can maintain natural eye contact while recording. A TV monitor gives you a live preview to check framing, posture, and lighting before you hit record.

The studio is fully self-service. Camera settings, lighting, and audio routing are preset. Walk in, press record, and focus on your conversation. At the end of your session, you'll leave with clean audio and video files ready for editing or immediate posting.
